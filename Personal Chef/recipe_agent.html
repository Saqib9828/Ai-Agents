Late Night Snack: Quick Spicy Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients You Have:

  • Olive Oil
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Salt
  • Red Chilly Powder
  • Sugar
  • Mustard Oil
  • Honey
  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Mango Juice
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lime
  • Frozen Chicken

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Prepare Chicken: Thaw frozen chicken if necessary. Cut into small pieces.
  2. Cook Eggs: Heat olive oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Set aside.
  3. Stir-fry Onion & Tomato: Add olive and mustard oil to the pan. Cook onions until golden, add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Cook Chicken: Add chicken, cook until golden and fully cooked (about 8-10 minutes). Season with turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.
  5. Mix Yogurt & Honey: Whisk yogurt with honey, lime juice, and a pinch of sugar. Add to the pan and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Finish: Add scrambled eggs back, mix well, adjust seasoning, and serve.
